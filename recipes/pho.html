Pho

Description

Pho is one of the most delish kind of Vietnamese Soup

Which is a fav bowls that each Vietnamese used to at least have it once in a lifetime due to its hot kind of broth will many healthy ingredients inside.

Ingredients

  • lemon
  • beef
  • cinamon
  • Pho

Steps

  1. Take the water and boil it with garlic,lemon,beef backbone/li>
  2. boil over the Pho then pour out the broth into Pho on each bowl
  3. add some meatball,slices of beef,lemon,sauces,chillies and some veggies on it
  4. Eat it!!
